About Radiant High School

Radiant High School, Makthal is committed to providing quality education with discipline and strong moral values. Our aim is to build confident students with academic excellence and life skills.

We focus on holistic development through modern teaching methods, individual care, and continuous assessment.

Facilities & Student Support

  • Bus facility available
  • Smart classrooms with digital content
  • Extra-curricular activities: Craft, Painting, Karate, Spoken English
  • Minority scholarships available
  • Medical practitioner support in school
  • Counselling by psychologists for life skills
